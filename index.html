Our Mission

San Joaquin Valley Town Hall is a non-profit organization run by an all-volunteer board of directors. Our mission is to bring nationally and internationally known, thought-provoking speakers who inform, educate, and entertain our audience.

Many members see Town Hall as a gift of knowledge they share with their families year after year.
